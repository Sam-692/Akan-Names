Akan Naming

The Akan naming was founded by the people of Ghana who frequently named their children after the day of the week they were born and
the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character
of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
